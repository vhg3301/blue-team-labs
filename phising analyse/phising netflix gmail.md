# Phishing Email Analysis — "GSOC Unauthorized Access / Netflix Help Center"

**Author:** Vitor  
**Date:** 2026-02-04  
**Purpose:** Analyze a phishing-style email claiming unauthorized access to a Netflix account. Identify social engineering techniques, technical indicators, and defensive recommendations.

---

## 1. Objective
This report aims to document and analyze a phishing email targeting Netflix users, highlighting the psychological and technical indicators of compromise (IOCs). The goal is to improve awareness and defensive measures.

---

## 2. Context
The email claims to originate from a Global Security Operations Center (GSOC) and mixes enterprise-like language with a consumer brand (“Netflix help center”). The attack attempts to create urgency and force the user to click a link to "recover" the account.
